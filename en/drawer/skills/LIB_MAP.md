- docs/MEW-ZH/, docs/MEA/, docs/LENIN/ → Chinese

MARX & ENGELS:
  German (no letters) — docs/MEW-ZENO/ (MEW German, Zeno.org; prefer for long works), docs/MEW/1/…25/, 40/, 42/ (MEW German, mlwerke.de), docs/MEW/default.html (chronological index of famous works).
  English — en/MECW/ (MECW 50 vols; no subdirectories; subdir must be en/MECW/).
  Chinese (CPC Translations Bureau) — docs/MEW-ZH/1/…50/ (Marx-Engels Collected Works 1st ed., vols 1–25 largely parallel MEW), docs/MEW-ZH/MEAW-1.html…4.html (Selected Works 1972 ed., vols 1–4, index files pointing into MEW-ZH), docs/MEA/1/…10/ (Marx-Engels Anthology, new translations). Files usually note the original source location in MEW and the source language (German/English/Russian etc.) at the end.

LENIN:
  Russian — ru/VIL-FB2/1/…42/ (5th Russian ed.; lacks post-1921 works, no notebooks or letters vols; preferred for major works), ru/VIL-UAIO/1/…55/ (5th Russian ed., all 55 vols; preferred for late works, letters, notebooks, annotations).
  Chinese (CPC Translations Bureau) — docs/LENIN/1/…60/ (Lenin Collected Works 2nd ed., 60 vols: vols 1–27 correspond to Russian 5th ed. vols 1–27; vols 28–43 to Russian 30–45; vols 44–53 are partial letters/telegrams corresponding to Russian 46–55; vols 54–55 correspond to Russian 28–29; vols 56–60 are additions unique to the Chinese edition), docs/LENIN/ls-1.html…4.html (Lenin Selected Works, vols 1–4). Files usually note the original source location and language at the end.
